About Emily Frels

Emily was born and raised in Atlanta, GA. She grew up in the arts with a focus on dance. After high school, Emily attended the University of Georgia (GO DAWGS!) and was a member of the Georgettes Danceline for 3 years.

After college, Emily lived in Atlanta for a bit and then began her journey through the US. Some cities of residence include Houston, Las Vegas, and Nashville. While living in Las Vegas, she met her husband (thanks eHarmony!), and soon moved to MN where she resides with her husband Blake, and their two dogs, Lilly and Gurley.
